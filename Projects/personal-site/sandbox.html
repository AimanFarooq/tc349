Media Sandbox

I am the Director of Social Media Marketing for the Media Sandbox Program here at Michigan State

After taking JRN 492 this summer with Karl Gude this past summer I was offered a position with Media Sandbox. Starting shortly before the beginning of the fall semester last year I took over the Director of Social Media Marketing and I have been the main driving force behind Sandbox's social media outlets, though I generally try to keep it a "ghost-written" medium to maintain the image of Sandbox. Since I've taken over I have more than doubled the audience on Facebook and greatly increased our audience on Twitter. The level of interaction and outreach has been greatly improved as well. I originally signed on for a trial basis but it has since become my main job here at the University. Below are some things I have done in this position:

  • Created brand awareness and a sense of community for The Media Sandbox
  • Coordinate daily posts on both Twitter and Facebook
  • Seek outreach opportunities
  • Facilitated initial contact and helped to plan out a guest speaking engagement with a Community Manager at Google
  • Worked with a team from CAS on a regular basis to create and implement strategies for the college for outreach and recruitment
