ASUS CampusLife

I am one of the Team Leaders for the ASUS Student Rep Program

I began as a rep for ASUS CampusLife in Fall 2011 after an interesting process of trying to get an interview. I finally managed to get an interview after harassing the hiring manager until I could finally get his attention. I literally almost showed up at the door of his office before he finally gave me a phone interview where I finally got the chance to prove that I would be an invaluable asset to the program. I have been a part of some significant projects as part of the program and I was asked to head up on of the teams for the program starting in the Summer of 2012. Here are a few of the things I have done while in this position:

  • Hosted events on the MSU Campus to create ASUS Brand Awareness
  • Paid for my own trip to CES 2012 to show that student created video and blog content could be beneficial to the brand.
  • Wrote for the student blog. My content can be found here
  • Began as head of the Blogger Team in Summer 2012
  • Was invited (paid) to CES 2013 to continue creating content like I had at CES 2012
